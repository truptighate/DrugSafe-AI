About the project

What is DrugSafe AI?

DrugSafe AI is a machine learning system that predicts the severity of drug-drug interactions (DDIs). It combines gradient boosting with SHAP explainability and LLM-generated clinical reasoning to help clinicians and researchers make safer decisions.

How it works

The model takes two drug names as input, encodes their pharmacological features, and predicts interaction risk across three severity levels. A SHAP explainer surfaces the top contributing features, and an LLM generates a plain-language clinical explanation.

Risk levels

Minor
Clinically insignificant. No action typically required.
Moderate
May require monitoring or dose adjustment.
Major
Potentially life-threatening. Avoid or manage closely.
